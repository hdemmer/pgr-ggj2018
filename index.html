The Paper Boat That Rocked
Global Game Jam 2018

Hendrik Demmer
Ivan Garde
Alexander Shestakov
Tommaso Piazza

Live the life of a pirate radio boat. Navigate through an archipelago where music is forbidden and turn on the transmission! Cities will react in different ways to the gift of music you are bringing In the Paper Boat that Rocked your goal is to awaken the musical talent dormant in the archipelago population. Getting close to the city allows your radio waves to reach them, transmit for long enough and will either find a musician to build a new awesome band or you will enrage the authorities that will pursue you The goal is to find all band members before the authorities catch you and end your illegal radio operations
